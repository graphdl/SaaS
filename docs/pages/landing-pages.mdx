# Landing Pages

Landing Pages are one of the most important aspects of marketing any SaaS, as they are the first 
point of contact for potential customers. They are the first impression that a potential 
customer will have of your product, and they are the first step in the sales funnel. 
A well-designed landing page can help you convert more visitors into customers, and a 
poorly-designed landing page can turn away potential customers.

One of the key metrics of any SaaS is the conversion rate, which is the percentage of
visitors to your landing page that convert into customers. A high conversion rate is
a sign that your landing page is well-designed and is doing its job of converting
visitors into customers.
